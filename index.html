What’s guaranteed

Woodwork guarantee & variations

Our guarantee

  • Woodwork guarantee applies when we perform full strip-to-bare plus our full coating system.
  • If you request a paint-over without full strip, adhesion/peeling is excluded and recorded on a signed Variation.
  • Health & safety controls (dust extraction, RPE, guarding) are mandatory and can’t be waived.

Opt-Out disclosure

If you prefer different methods or products (e.g., solvent systems), we’ll prepare a simple Opt-Out for signature so scope, price, and warranty are clear.

How we work safely

Safety & Environment

  • Low-VOC paints by default (eco-friendly).

FAQs

Can I supply my own paints and materials?

Yes. We can supply everything once colours/spec are confirmed, or you can provide your own. Prices adjust accordingly — we’ll quote labour-only or hybrid. Day rates from £275 per painter are available for suitable day-work.

Do day rates include materials?

Day rates are labour-only unless agreed otherwise. We can include materials at cost in a written quote, or you can supply them.

How does this affect the guarantee?

Our full woodwork guarantee applies when we complete a full strip-to-bare plus our full system. If you request a paint-over or non-standard system, adhesion/peeling is excluded and we’ll record the change on a signed Opt-Out/Variation. Health & safety controls remain mandatory.
